About Medicine Explorer

Disclaimer: The Medicine Explorer Application is a Beta Research Project Prototype and not for clinical use.


Medicine Explorer is for the general public and clinicians who seek drug information (over the counter and prescription) for making better health decisions. The site uses datasets from openFDA to provide easy access to public data relating to adverse events, product labeling, and recall enforcement reports.

General Public example use

You pick up a decongestant from the pharmacy but remember that some may interact negatively with your high blood pressure prescription. With Medicine Explorer, you can easily search on your blood pressure medication and navigate to the drug interaction information and scan the results for interaction with over the counter medications. Also, you can see the potential side effects of the medications to make an informed decision.


Clinician example use

As a doctor, use Medicine Explorer to help decide which over the counter medications would be safe for your patients to take. Through the site, you have the option to view adverse reaction data regarding medications. While reviewing reported issues, you also have the capability to inspect At Risk Population data and see if a drug is safe to prescribe to patients who are pregnant. Allowing you to be assure that the medicine is safe with low risk of complications for your patients.
